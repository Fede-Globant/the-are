Building the Future of Life-Changing Innovation

Alexandria Real Estate Equities, INC. creates unsurpassed spaces for innovative companies and mission-critical research institutions in the life science and technology sectors. Alexandria is the largest and leading real estate investment trust uniquely focused on collaborative science and technology campuses in urban innovation clusters. As a true partner to its high-quality tenants and expansive network, Alexandria has a preeminent market presence in key locations, including San Diego, Greater Boston, San Francisco, New York City, Seattle, Maryland, and Research Triangle Park.

Alexandria is about more than real estate. It’s about the way Alexandria creates its properties and connects people within them. Alexandria is at the forefront of enhancing the ability of its tenants to recruit and retain world-class talent and inspire collaboration, efficiency, creativity, and success.
